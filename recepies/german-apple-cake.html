Description

Cacio e Pepe means "cheese and pepper" from italian. It has a smooth, creme texture, and it is not difficult to make.

Still, you need to be a bit careful, as the cheese can become lumpy.

Ingredients(4 servings)

  • 450g spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • 2 minced glove of garlic
  • 1 table spoon ground black pepper
  • 200g pecorino romano cheese
  • 2l pasta water

Steps

  1. Boil your spaghetti in salted water for about 8 minutes. Keep some of that pasta water!!!
  2. Grate a picorino cheese.
  3. Season chickine breasts on both
  4. Place flour, beaten eggs, and panko into separate dishes.
  5. Coat chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess.
  6. Dip into egg, and then press onto panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  7. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in the hot oil.
  8. Fry until golden brown, 3 or 4 minutes on each side. Transfer to a plate with the paper towel. Let the oil drain.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
