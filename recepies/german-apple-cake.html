German Apple Cake

Photo by 4028mdk09, licensed under CC BY-SA 3.0 .

Description

German Apple Cake is a simple and delicious desert, with plenty of apple chunks, lots of fall spices and crunchy peanuts.

Ingredients (16 servings)

  • 400g sugar
  • 240ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 large eggs
  • 500g tart apples, peeled and finely chopped
  • 100 g chopped pecans

Steps

  1. 180°C conventional heator or 160°C fan. Grease and lightly flour a 24cm springform pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together sugar, oil, vanilla, and eggs for 2 to 3 minutes until smooth and slightly creamy.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until combined.
  5. Fold in chopped apples and pecans.The batter will be thick - that's normal.
  6. Spread batter into the pan.Bake for 50 to 60 minutes. Check with a toothpick - it should come out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then remove and cool completely.
  8. Dust with powdered sugar (optional).
  9. Enjoy!
