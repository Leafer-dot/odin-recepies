Cacio e Pepe

Description

Chicken Katsu is a simple Japanese dish made of deep-fried chicken coated in panko (japanese breadcrumbs).

It can be served with white rice or shredded lettuce and tonkatsu sauce, which you can buy at Asian supermarkets.

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 450g spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • 2 minced glove of garlic
  • 1 table spoon ground black pepper
  • 200g pecorino romano cheese
  • 2l pasta water

Steps

  1. Boil your spaghetti in salted water for about 8 minutes. Keep some of that pasta water!!!
  2. Grate a picorino cheese.
  3. Add 3-4 tablespoons of pasta water, and mix the cheese until creamy consistancy.
  4. Toast ground pepper in a pan on a low heat for about 30 seconds.
  5. Add a ladle of pasta water to your pan and let it simmer for a couple of minutes.
  6. Add the pasta and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
  7. Take you pasta of the heat, add the pecorino creme and mix it.
  8. Plate the pasta, then finish with extra sauce and a sprinkle of Pecorino.
  9. Enjoy!
